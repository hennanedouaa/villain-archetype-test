The Three Villain Archetypes

The Bitch

The Mastermind: Strategic and self-serving, this archetype is driven by personal goals and will navigate any obstacle to achieve them. Their actions are not personal but are calculated steps towards their ambitions.

The Whore

The Chaos Creator: Thrives on creating discord and enjoys the turmoil they cause. They act out of spite and find pleasure in disrupting the peace, often without a clear motive.

The Slut

The Attention Seeker: Desperate for validation, this archetype will adapt their behavior to gain approval. They may engage in negative actions to secure their place in the spotlight or someone's affection.

Archetype Descriptions

Bitch – The Mastermind Villain

They are the architects of their own destiny. Always thinking five steps ahead, they will stop at nothing to achieve their goals — even if it means stepping over anyone in their way. For them, it's never personal; it's simply the art of winning.

Whore – The Chaos Villain

They were born to stir the pot. Drama follows them not because they need it, but because they can. They speak without a filter, throw shade with precision, and ensure no one ever forgets their presence. Their cruelty isn't strategic — it's instinct.

Slut – The Manipulative Villain

They know how to work a room, charm their way into hearts, and keep themselves in the spotlight. But beneath the charm lies a desperate need for validation. They bend their morals, align with power, and join in on cruelty if it means protecting their place in someone's heart — or staying at the center of attention.
